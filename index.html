What We Do

Hanlen Law Group is a firm dedicated to helping our clients navigate through some of the most challenging times of their lives. Our promise is to make the process easier on you and to help you come out the other side feeling better. The firm offers knowledgeable attorneys who are not just thinking about winning your case or solving your matter, but also taking the necessary steps to make sure your long-term goals are considered and achieved. Whether it's navigating a difficult divorce or complex custody case, negotiating a new business agreement, or arguing for the benefits your deserve, you'll be treated with respect, courtesy, empathy as we advocate for your rights and best interests. Meet the team

We'd love to talk with you about your legal needs.

Jason Hanlen

Founder

Jason earned his law degree from Temple University's Beasley School of Law. He later attended St. Joseph's University Haub School of Business where he earned an Executive MBA and expanded his knowledge of business.

Besides founding Hanlen Law Group, Jason is also a member of the Philadelphia Bar Association, Barrister's Association of Philadelphia, and the Nicolas Cipriani Bar Association of Family Law Attorneys. Jason does pro bono legal work through the Philadelphia Volunteers for the Indigent Program. He also enjoys teaching entrepreneurial skills to children and young adults through his non-profit organization. He is also a business coach and consultant.

Jamie Ross

Lawyer

Jamie earned bachelor's degree, cum laude, and law degree from Temple University and Temple's Beasley School of Law. Jamie is licensed to practice in Pennsylvania.

She has maintained a diverse and rewarding legal career, practicing in the corporate and private sectors, as well as more recently with the Federal Government. She has handled a variety of legal matters including representing hundreds of clients at administrative hearings; handling contracts; business law, family law; and personal injury matters. She brings with her a strong skill set and a wealth of legal experience and exposure.

Bob Pearson

Lawyer

Attorney Bob Pearson is a native of Hanover, Pennsylvania and a graduate of Delone Catholic High School. Attorney Pearson earned a Bachelor's degree in psychology from Saint Joseph's University in 2015, and his law degree from Widener University Commonwealth Law School in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania in 2015. Attorney Pearson focuses his practice of law in Family Law (Divorce and Custody).
