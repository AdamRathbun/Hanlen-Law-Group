How we can help with your business contracts

It's time to get your business back on track! We currently offer many solutions including for-profit and non-profit organization, partnership agreements, independent contractor agreements and more.

Independent Contractor Agreements: It is very important that you have an agreement with your independent contractors that adheres to the laws of your state. It can be fairly easy to inadvertently turn an independent contractor into an employee. A clear contract can help you avoid this issue. We offer competitive rates for independent contractor agreements tailored specifically to your business and industry.

Vendor Agreements: Do you currently work with vendors? You having an agreement can make it clear what the expectations and obligations for each party. Do you need a review of your agreement? Contact us today to discuss customizable vendor agreements, review of agreements, as well as enforcement of agreements.

Employment Agrements & Handbooks: Employment agreements can clarify the terms of employment including salary, severance in the event of termiantion, benefits, etc. However, it may not always be a good idea to have an employement agreement. Similarly, handbooks can be one of the most valuable docuemnts with regard to your employees. If you have employees or are planning to hire employees, talk to us today about crafting an employee handbook that fits your business needs.
